Sudokuban 3D

This is a remake/sequel to a 2D puzzle game i designed and made with a friend in 2017.
I'm working on this actively as a hobby,
hoping to release it one day on itch/steam.

How to play?

WASD/Arrows for movement.

The mechanics of the game is sokoban (push boxes to goals/points),
and at the same time sudoku-rules apply.

As an example, you can't move a blue box onto
the horizontal or vertical grid path of another blue one.

Extra

This is very early gameplay,
the goal is before 2020 to have
a 10 minute demo of the game running,
which showcases the beginning of the start-area
and some of the later mechanics
